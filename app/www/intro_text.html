WELCOME TO MULTIOVIZ

An interactive open source software tool for assessment of gene regulatory networks (GRNs).

Multioviz enables the visualization of significant molecular variables in cellular processes as GRNs. It facilitates in-silico hypothesis testing through GRN perturbation.

 WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH MULTIOVIZ:

  • Integrate any computational model that performs feature selection and prioritization.
  • Visualize the prioritized molecular features of up to two molecular levels (e.g. genes, single nucleotide polymorphisms) and the interactions of features within and between molecular levels.
  • Perturb features and interactions to test in-silico how the cellular process would behave. 
  • Threshold features by statistical score. 
  • Choose GRN layout.
  • Download visualization.
